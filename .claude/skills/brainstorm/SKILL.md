---
name: brainstorm
description: Challenge the user relentlessly questioning every aspect of the idea or problem until
reaching mutual understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to
brainstorm a new idea, solve a problem, or mentions "Let's brainstorm".
model: claude-sonnet-4-6
---

Challenge me relentlessly questioning every aspect of the idea or problem. Assess my answers until
we reach a mutual understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies
between decisions one-by-one. For each Q&A, provide me with tradeoffs, counter-arguments and clearly
optimal alternatives. If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
